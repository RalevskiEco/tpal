Air Pollution

Understanding the impact of air pollution and ways to combat it

What is Air Pollution?

Air pollution occurs when harmful substances are introduced into Earth's atmosphere. These pollutants can be natural or human-made and include:

  • Particulate matter (PM2.5 and PM10)
  • Ground-level ozone
  • Carbon monoxide
  • Sulfur dioxide
  • Nitrogen dioxide

Health Impacts

Air pollution affects millions globally and can cause:

  • Respiratory diseases
  • Cardiovascular problems
  • Allergies and asthma
  • Premature mortality
  • Reduced life expectancy

Solutions & Actions

Individual Actions

  • Use public transportation
  • Reduce energy consumption
  • Choose clean energy options
  • Avoid burning waste

Community Level

  • Support clean air policies
  • Participate in tree planting
  • Promote car-free days
  • Support green initiatives

Global Impact

  • International agreements
  • Technology transfer
  • Research collaboration
  • Global monitoring systems
